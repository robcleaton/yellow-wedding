General Instructions for guests; We recommend all guests enter the grounds via the London Road Gate. Please note that the Horniman Drive Gate is locked at dusk and no access is possible through this gate after that time (if you are attending a wedding at the Horniman Bandstand and will be leaving before dusk then you may also park on Horniman Drive if you prefer).

Free parking is available on surrounding residential roads, less than five minutes walk from the gate, in particular Wood Vale, Sydenham Rise & Sydenham Hill (see map).

Blue Badge Holders/Mobility Impaired Guests;

We are able to request parking spaces on a case-by-case basis for guests with mobility issues. These must be booked in advance. Please request further information from amanda@suzannejames.co.uk
