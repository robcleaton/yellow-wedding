We recommend all guests enter the grounds via the London Road Gate. Please note that the Horniman Drive Gate is locked at dusk and no access is possible through this gate after that time (if you are attending a wedding at the Horniman Bandstand and will be leaving before dusk then you may also park on Horniman Drive if you prefer).

Free parking is available on surrounding residential roads, less than five minutes walk from the gate, in particular Wood Vale, Sydenham Rise & Sydenham Hill (see map).

Blue Badge Holders/Mobility Impaired Guests;

We are able to request parking spaces on a case-by-case basis for guests with mobility issues. These must be booked in advance. Please request further information from amanda@suzannejames.co.uk

Crown & Greyhound

The Crown & Greyhound is an iconic South London pub that has 20 quality rooms in a leafy haven set in the heart of Dulwich Village, just a short distance from the Horniman Museum. All rooms include free wifi and a complimentary buffet breakfast.

The Victoria Inn

The bedrooms at the Victoria Inn are design-led without compromising on convenience or comfort. Fifteen beautifully appointed bedrooms with their own en suite bathrooms. There are four different room types to suit every need; standard double, twin superior double and feature double with a sofa-bed suitable for children, allowing families of up to four to stay together. Please speak to Jo (General Manager) and let him know you’ve come via Suzanne James and he will take extra good care of you!

Fox Hill Bed & Breakfast

This large, comfortable Victorian family house, is situated on Fox Hill in South London which descends from the Crystal Palace ridge. Fox Hill was immortalised by the French painter, Camille Pissarro who painted the view up the hill in 1870, and this can be seen in the National Gallery.

2.3 miles
The Melrose Guest House

A family owned and managed guest house, Melrose House is maintained and approved to the very high standards required of a 4 Star Guest House. Situated in the quiet suburb of Beckenham/Penge, Melrose House is a mere 15-minute train journey from the centre of London, but still remote enough to escape the noise, bustle and high prices of central London. Offers guests off street parking, en suite bedrooms, period breakfast room and a lounge/conservatory that accesses directly into the relaxing mixed flower, rockery and pond garden. Notes: All prices include breakfast. Minimum 2-night stay
